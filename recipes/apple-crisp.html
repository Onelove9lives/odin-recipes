Mini Apple Crisp Skillet Recipt

Say hello to these adorable Mini Apple Crisp Skillets, perfect for satisfying your sweet tooth without going overboard!.

With a warm, cinnamon-spiced apple filling and a crispy oat topping, these personal-sized treats are sure to be a hit at your next gathering.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup spice cake mix
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, divided
  • 2 apples, peeled, cored, and sliced

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375˚F (190˚C).
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the cake mix and 2 tablespoons of melted
  3. butter until the mixture is crumbly.
  4. Pour 1 tablespoon of melted butter into the bottom of two small cast-iron
  5. skillets.
  6. Arrange the apple slices in each skillet, ensuring the bottom is covered.
  7. Sprinkle cake mixture over the apples.
  8. Bake for 20 minutes, or until top appears crispy and edges are bubbling.
  9. Cool for 10 minutes.
